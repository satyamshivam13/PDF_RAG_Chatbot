Welcome to SmartSync – your intelligent file backup and sync solution!

SmartSync helps users automatically back up important files across devices in real-time. It supports Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms.

Key Features:
- Real-time sync across devices
- Encrypted cloud backups
- Version history and file recovery
- Integration with Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive
- Bandwidth throttling and scheduling options

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How do I install SmartSync?
A: Visit our website at www.smartsync.io and download the installer for your operating system. Follow the setup wizard to complete the installation.

Q: How do I restore a deleted file?
A: Go to the SmartSync dashboard, navigate to the folder, click “Version History,” and select the file you want to restore.

Q: Is my data encrypted?
A: Yes, SmartSync uses end-to-end AES-256 encryption to ensure your files are safe and private.

Q: What happens if I lose internet connection?
A: SmartSync will queue your changes locally and sync them automatically once you're back online.

Support:
For troubleshooting, visit our help center or contact support at support@smartsync.io.

Pro Tip:
You can exclude temporary or large files by adding them to your SmartSync Ignore List in the app settings.
